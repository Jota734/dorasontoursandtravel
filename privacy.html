Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025


At Dorason Tours, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.


1. Information We Collect

We collect your name, email, and phone number only when you fill out our booking form or contact us.


2. How We Use Information

We use your data solely to arrange your safari, secure permits (Gorilla/Chimp), and communicate regarding your trip.


3. Photo Usage

Photos taken during community tours may be used on our website gallery. If you prefer not to be photographed, please inform your guide.
