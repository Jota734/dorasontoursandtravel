Tourism with a Purpose

Dorason Tours was founded on the principle that the community must benefit from the beauty of Lake Bunyonyi. We actively support orphans, widows, and local schools.

School Fees

We sponsor education for 10 orphans annually.

Church Support

Helping renovate local community churches.

Direct Aid

Providing soap, sugar, and clothes to the needy.
