Last Updated: 2025


1. Bookings & Deposits

To confirm a safari, a 30% deposit is required. The balance must be paid before the start of the tour. Gorilla permits must be paid for in full at the time of booking.


2. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made 30 days before arrival will receive a 70% refund (excluding permit fees). Cancellations less than 30 days are non-refundable.

Dorason Tours is not liable for personal injury, loss of luggage, or unforeseen delays caused by weather or traffic.
